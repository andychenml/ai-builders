About NYC AI Builders

Since its founding in August 2022, NYC AI Builders has been a dedicated platform for meaningful dialogues in the field of AI development. It primarily connects professionals based in NYC while also extending its reach globally. The Slack group "AI Builders" has a membership of approximately 500 individuals, including AI developers, entrepreneurs, researchers, and industry experts. Around two-thirds of these members are NYC-based, with the remainder hailing from diverse global locations.

Conversations within this community adhere to the Chatham House Rule, ensuring that information can be shared freely while keeping the identity and affiliation of contributors confidential.
