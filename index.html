About NYC AI Builders

Since its inception in August 2022, NYC AI Builders has served as a dedicated forum for substantive dialogues in the realm of AI development, predominantly connecting professionals in NYC and extending globally. Each year, approximately 500 AI developers, entrepreneurs, researchers, and industry experts join our Slack group, "AI Builders." Roughly two-thirds of our members are based in NYC, with the rest contributing from various locations worldwide; the majority are from technology sectors, while others represent academia, research, and business.

Discussions within this community are conducted under the Chatham House Rule, ensuring that information can be freely used but the identity and affiliation of the contributors remain confidential. This setting allows participants to engage without the constraints often imposed by their professional roles, fostering an atmosphere for genuine learning, reflection, and insight. Unlike traditional forums, NYC AI Builders does not operate under a rigid agenda, allowing for organic and timely discussions to flourish. No resolutions are proposed, no votes are taken, and no official statements are issued.
